Fun Facts About Coffee

  • Most Popular Drink: Coffee is one of the most popular drinks worldwide, with over 2 billion cups consumed each day.
  • Coffee Beans are Seeds: What we call coffee "beans" are actually seeds that come from a fruit called a coffee cherry, which grows on the coffee plant.
  • Caffeine Power: The caffeine in coffee blocks adenosine, a chemical that makes you feel tired, helping you feel more alert.
